My story

Welcome to Tres Hombres Steak House & Seafoods

Where the ocean’s freshest bounty meets the finest meats from our very own farm. At Tres Hombres, we pride ourselves on serving vibrant, flavorful seafood and premium cuts of beef grilled to perfection.

Our signature masterpiece — the Italian Beef Steak made from Japanese Wagyu — is a true labor of love. Raised on the lush pastures of Tres Hombres Farm, our Wagyu cattle are cared for with the highest standards, producing marbled, melt-in-your-mouth beef unlike anything you’ve tasted before. Expertly seasoned and slow-grilled over aromatic hardwood, this dish delivers an unforgettable harmony of Italian culinary tradition and Japanese craftsmanship.

Pair it with our lobster tails, shrimp platters, or fresh-catch salmon for a feast that celebrates both land and sea — all served with passion in a warm, inviting atmosphere.

One bite of our signature Wagyu Italian Beef Steak is a journey to heaven — a melt-in-your-mouth experience you’ll find only here at Tres Hombres.
