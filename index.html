About Me

From the Middle of the Ocean to the Heart of the Digital World

For over a decade, I sailed across oceans—living a life many dream of, yet few truly understand. I was a seafarer, an officer, navigating ships through storms and silence, sunrise and solitude. I traveled the world, docked in countless ports, and witnessed breathtaking horizons that most only see in postcards.

But after years of chasing sunsets at sea, I realized something:
What many call "a vacation," I called "coming home."

So I made the hardest decision of my life—
To retire from the waves and return to the shore, not just physically, but entirely.
To live not just between contracts, but every single day—fully present, finally grounded.

Today, I’ve traded my uniform for creativity and connection.
I’ve transformed my discipline and precision at sea into a diverse set of digital skills:

  • Social Media Manager
  • Video Editor/Content Creator
  • Executive Support Assistant
  • Graphic Designer
  • Graphic Artist
  • Self-Taught Web Developer

And this website?
I built it myself—from scratch, without spending a single penny—just pure effort, late nights, and a vision for a new chapter.

Welcome to my digital harbor—
where experience meets reinvention,
and where a former seafarer finally chooses to anchor on land.
